Museum of Candy
Welcome to the Museum of Candy, where you can satisfy your sweet tooth and learn about the history of one of the world's most beloved treats. As you enter the museum, you are greeted by the delicious aroma of chocolate, caramel, and fruity flavors. The first exhibit showcases the origins of candy, dating back to ancient civilizations like the Egyptians and Aztecs, who used honey and other natural ingredients to make sweet treats.
Discover More
Moving through the museum, you'll discover how candy has evolved over time, from the first hard candies in the 17th century to the mass production of candy in the 20th century. The centerpiece of the museum is the Candy Kitchen, where you can watch skilled candy makers create delicious treats right before your eyes. You'll see the process of melting and molding chocolate, and watch as lollipops and other candies are handcrafted with precision and care.
Sweet Shop
Our Sweet Shop is a treasure trove of classic and nostalgic candies.We also carry a variety of handmade chocolates, featuring unique flavors like lavender and sea salt, as well as seasonal and holiday-themed treats. In addition to our wide selection of candies, we also offer a range of candy-themed merchandise, including t-shirts, mugs, and even candy-scented candles. The Sweet Shop at the Museum of Candy is the perfect place to indulge in your favorite treats, discover new favorites, and take home a piece of candy history. So come on in, grab a bag or two, and satisfy your sweet tooth today!
